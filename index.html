Who am I

As an Artist

I am both a traditional and digital artist which started as doing illustrations in my free time until around high school which was when I started taking actual art classes. I took three consecutive drawing classes and a graphics design class. Based off of those experiences, I am now interested in exploring potential careers in the graphics design field. I have experience using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere as well as programs outside of the Adobe Suite such as Clip Studio Paint. I am currently enrolled in the University of Connecticut where I am aiming for a Bachelor's in Digital Media and Design and expect to graduate in the year 2020.
